SOS — Setup & Activation
Setup: ⚙ Settings → Emergency tab → enter caregiver phone → Save.
Activate Hold eyes closed for 10 seconds from any screen — red bar fills. Or blink-scan to Emergency → 🆘 SOS button.
App fetches GPS location and opens SMS with Google Maps link. Repeats every 30s until cancelled.
SMS works on mobile. On PC: message copied to clipboard automatically.
